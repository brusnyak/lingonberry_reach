# Neighborly Outreach DraftS - UK Trades

These drafts follow the "friendly neighbor" vibe: plain, simple, direct, and short. No prices, no free trials, no "salesy" language.

---

## Template 1: The "Simple Help" (Direct & Short)

**Subject:** quick question
**Email:**

```
Hey [Name],

I build automations that save trade businesses a few hours of admin every week. 

If I can help you reply to enquiries faster and qualify leads automatically, would you be interested?

Cheers, [Sender]
```

---

## Rationale (Based on /material/ and VALUES.md)

1. **Plain Text Only:** No HTML, no links, no tracking pixels. Looks like a "normal" email from a person.
2. **Short Word Count:** Every draft is under 50 words. Perfect for mobile reading.
3. **Low Friction CTA:** Asks for interest/curiosity rather than a meeting or a sale.
4. **Disarming Tone:** "I'm starting out" or "I build small tools" feels helpful and neighborly, not corporate or pushing a "system".
5. **No Mention of AI:** Focuses on the *outcome* (saving time, qualifying leads) rather than the *technology* (AI, software), which can be off-putting to some tradesmen.
