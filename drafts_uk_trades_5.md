## Summary of Approach

**Copy formula used:**
1. **Hook:** Simple AI that handles [specific pain point]
2. **Value:** Filter junk → draft quotes → book jobs → you only see hot ones
3. **Trust:** Runs in your tools, you approve everything, GDPR-safe
4. **Offer:** €497 intro price + testimonial exchange
5. **Risk reversal:** 7-day free test, zero commitment
6. **CTA:** Simple yes/no response (low friction)

**Why this works for UK trades:**
- They get flooded with leads from Checkatrade, MyBuilder, etc.
- They hate admin and wasting time on weak enquiries
- They respond well to direct, no-nonsense copy
- Low-commitment CTA (yes/no) gets more replies than asking for calls
